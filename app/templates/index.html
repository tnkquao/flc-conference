Experience The First Love Conference

The First Love Conference is a spirit filled international event that brings together young people and adults from over 49 nations, united by a passion for Jesus and a desire to experience His presence in a deeper way.

With thousands joining from America, Europe, the Caribbean, Australia, New Zealand, and across Africa, this electrifying gathering features powerful worship, transformational teachings, and an atmosphere charged with inspiration.

Join globally renowned speakers and a diverse community as we explore this year's theme, “THE LORD IS THERE”—a call to experience His presence, power, and purpose like never before.

This is more than a conference—it's an encounter with His presence. See you there!

-Joshua Heward-Mills, Pastor of The First Love Church

Speakers

Joshua Heward-Mills

Conference Host and Lead Pastor, First Love Church

Dag Heward-Mills

Dag Heward-Mills Ministries, Ghana

About

Dag Heward-Mills is an evangelist, pastor, author, and missionary known for his global impact in ministry.
He is the founder of the United Denominations Originating from the Lighthouse Group of Churches (UD-OLGC), a denomination with thousands of churches in over 90 countries.
He also launched the Healing Jesus Campaign, a series of large-scale gospel crusades that have reached millions across Africa. In addition to planting churches and organizing crusades, he has authored over 90 influential books on leadership, ministry, and church growth.
Bishop Dag continues to train missionaries and raise leaders with a strong focus on the Great Commission

Ben Fitzgerald

Awakening Europe, Germany

About

Ben Fitzgerald is the senior leader of Awakening Europe, a movement dedicated to spreading the gospel across Europe through stadium events, city-wide outreaches, and training schools.
Currently based in Germany, Ben leads the Awakening Team and travels extensively, preaching the message of Jesus and equipping believers to live in the fullness of freedom and identity found in Christ.
With a deep conviction that the future of nations will be transformed by radical believers, Ben’s passion is to see people set free and boldly proclaiming the Kingdom of God.

Joshua McCauley

Redemption Church and Rhema, South Africa

About

Joshua McCauley is the Senior Pastor of Rhema and Redemption Church Johannesburg, two vibrant and rapidly growing congregations in South Africa. Known for his passionate and engaging teaching, he is a prominent voice in the global grace movement. His messages focus on the transformative power of God’s love and the finished work of Jesus, helping believers experience true freedom, healing, and peace in Christ. Pastor Joshua’s approach to scripture brings fresh insights, often using typology and the original Hebrew text to highlight the depth of God’s goodness.

Glyn Barrett

Audacious, UK and National leader of Assemblies of God UK

About

Glyn Barrett is a pastor, international speaker, and author known for his dynamic preaching and inspiring leadership. He leads !Audacious Church, a thriving multi-site church with campuses across the UK and Europe.
Glyn currently serves as the National Leader of Assemblies of God Great Britain, Chair of Empowered21 of Europe, and Global Church Planting Chair for AoG.
Passionate about raising leaders and planting churches, he continues to speak around the world while pursuing a Doctorate in Leadership and Global Perspectives.

David Diga Hernandez

David Diga Hernandez Ministries, USA

About

David Diga Hernandez is an evangelist, bestselling author, YouTuber, and healing minister with a deep friendship with the Holy Spirit. His ministry hosts powerful encounter services around the world and reaches millions through online media.
David has a heart for the lost and a strong desire to help this generation connect with the Holy Spirit in a real and personal way. Through his ministry, many have come to know Jesus, experienced healing and freedom, and discovered a deeper walk with God.
He carries a special grace to lead people to Christ and to help believers grow closer to the Holy Spirit.

Daniel Chand

Walking Like Jesus Ministries, UK

About

Daniel Chand is an evangelist, pastor, author, and the founder of Walking Like Jesus Ministries.
He leads large-scale gospel campaigns, including Tent Revivals and international crusades, reaching hundreds of thousands of people globally. Through his ministry, Daniel also hosts weekly television programs on TBN UK, such as Let’s Talk Revival and Walking Like Jesus. Alongside his evangelistic work, Daniel and his wife Tanya pastor Pragma Church in Milton Keynes, a thriving congregation focused on revival and equipping believers. With a heart to reach the lost, Daniel has ministered in over 32 nations, hosting powerful crusades and spreading the message of salvation and healing. His ministry's mandate is to "reach the lost at any cost."

Markus Wenz

Holy Spirit Night Movement at the Gospel Forum, Germany

About

Markus Wenz is the leader of the Holy Spirit Night Movement and a key member of the leadership team at Gospel Forum Church in Stuttgart, Germany.
He is passionate about seeing a generation rise up to live in true freedom and walk in the supernatural power of the Holy Spirit. After a life-changing encounter with the Holy Spirit, Markus has made it his mission to create space for God’s presence and power to move. He is deeply committed to equipping and empowering young people to bring transformation in every sphere of society—through bold faith, spiritual authority, and a lifestyle led by the Spirit.

Teo Hayashi

Dunamis Movement and Monte Sião Church, Brazil

About

Teo Hayashi is the founder of the Dunamis Movement and a passionate leader focused on equipping a generation to bring the Kingdom of Heaven to Earth.
He serves as a writer, speaker, and senior pastor at Monte Sião Church in São Paulo, Brazil. His ministry journey began with Youth With A Mission (YWAM) and continued as a youth pastor in North Carolina.
After returning to Brazil, Teo envisioned a move of God among college students—one that would spark revival and global impact. That vision became the Dunamis Movement.

Kong Hee

City Harvest Church, Singapore

About

Kong Hee is the founder and senior pastor of City Harvest Church, a vibrant, multi-campus congregation. He also established the School of Theology, Church Without Walls, and City Harvest Community Services Association, equipping leaders and serving thousands in need.
A spiritual son of the late Dr. David Yonggi Cho, who led the world’s largest church, Kong carries a deep commitment to Pentecostal faith, spiritual formation, and Christlikeness.
He continues to champion soul-winning, church growth, and intimacy with God as the ultimate goal of every believer. He is currently pursuing an education in theology.
